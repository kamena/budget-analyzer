About

"Where did my money go?" is web app that will help you analyze the money you spend. Stop wasting money without limits, save money, buy that new car you want so much!

"Where did my money go?" will count your revenue and expenditure for you. "Where did my money go?" will tell you for what you are spending more money so you can see where you'll need to make changes in order to save money. "Where did my money go?" works for you for free so you won't need to pay for one more thing!
